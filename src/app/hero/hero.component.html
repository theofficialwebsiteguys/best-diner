Welcome to The Best Diner

Located in the heart of downtown Suffern. The Best Diner (TBD) is a family restaurant with bar. We have been an Indian restaurant since 1986. Starting April 2023, We decided to convert our Organic Indian menu to a full diner stype menu. We will be open 7 days a week from 7am to 9pm or 10pm (Fridays and Saturdays only). We will serve American style breakfast, lunch and dinner with a pinch of Spanish, Italian, and oriental cuisines. TBD is a casual yet elegant family restaurant, serving authentic cuisines, complimented by fine wine selections from around the world. Enjoy our indoor or outdoor dining options with friends and family. Looking forward to serving you soon.
